Steering Committee



Steering Committee members are appointed by the Provost and are chosen from distinguished faculty and prominent community members. They advise the Director on policy and on all matters that help promote the mission of the Center.

Tribute to Late Mildred Millie

The Humanities Center mourns the passing of Mildred "Millie" Jeffrey in March, 2004. Millie Jeffrey, Professor Emeritus was a longtime friend and supporter of the Humanities Center. She was a faithful and active member of the Center's Steering Committee since its inception, attending meetings and Center events without fail, and contributing much to the formation and continuation of the Humanities Center's mission and success.
